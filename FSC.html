About The Tournament

This program is a badminton championship specially organized for UPNM students, aiming to provide a dynamic platform for them to showcase their skills and talents in badminton. The championship also aims to strengthen relationships between students from various faculties and residential colleges through healthy competition and foster camaraderie among students. By combining elements of sports and education, the Fly Smash Championship not only gives participants the opportunity to improve their badminton performance but also promotes an active and healthy lifestyle among students. This championship has become a highly anticipated event where each match not only showcases players' excellence but also high sportsmanship spirit. Additionally, the Fly Smash Championship opens opportunities for sponsors and external communities to support youth sports development, thereby strengthening collaborative networks between the university and industry.
